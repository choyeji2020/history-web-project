Plot
Yeon Gaesomun, who is destined to change the world, is established in the village of Mountain Baekdu by his father. However, he run away to Silla. He takes Kim Yu-shin as his master and learns martial arts in 'Flower Knight'. When he goes to Sui Dynasty, he gets to know Lee Mi-il, but he is killed by Lee Se-min's army. So Yeon Gaesomun returns to Goguryeo. When King Bojang is crowned, the practical politics are supervised by him. Goguryeo under the command of Yeon Gaesomun has won consecutive victories from the Tang Dynasty. After Yeon Gaesomun retire form politics, Goguryeo surrender to the Tang Dynasty due to internal division.

View History with Yeon Gaesomun

1. Battle of Ansi Province

King Taejong invaded Goguryeo three times in total. Several castles on Goguryeo's defense lines were captured and battles broke out in the remaining Ansi Province. Ansi Castle, guarded by Yang Man-chun, consists of a fortress. Several wall attacks were made, but Goguryeo quickly defended the invasion. Eventually, the Chinese Dang army decided to attack by stacking mud mountains higher than the castle, but the soil mountain was also occupied by the Goguryeo army, so the chinese army could not withstand the cold and hunger and withdrew the war about in 80 days. Since then, the Chiness invasion has repeatedly failed, and the Dang Dynasty's invasion of Goguryeo ended with the death of Dang Tae-jong.

A fly in a ointment

1. Yeon Gaesomun was not Kim Yu-shin's servant

Kim Yu-shin and Yeon Gaesomun had two indirect relationships by record. Kim Chun-chu, a Goguryeo envoy caught in Silla in October 645, protested Kim Yu-shin's release with the military. In January 662, Dang troops crossed the border to supply food when they were starving around Pyongyang.

2. Makriji is not the highest government post (the government-backed system at the end of the Koguryo period).

In the drama, Makriji is described as the highest official position, but Makriji is the same official position as Tae Dae-hyung, who ranks second in the Goguryeo government official system. The first place has the greatest authority for generations.

3. It is fiction that Yeon Gaesomun was the subordinate of Lee Mil.

In the drama, Yeon Gaesomun takes Lee Mil as his master and marries his sister. Lee Mil lived from 582 to 618. In addition, his ancestors planned to assasin Suyangje as he was from Liaodong and was one of the military heroes at the end of the Sui Dynasty. Therefore, it is impossible to interpret and decide Lee Mil as a descendant of Goguryeo only with records and origins.

4. It is fiction that Yeon Gaesomun lived until the end of Goguryeo.

According to records, Yeon Gaesomun died in 666, and he did not live until the fall of Goguryeo in 668. In the drama, Yeon Gaesomun appeared until the last episode and until the day Goguryeo collapsed, which is not true.
