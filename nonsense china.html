What is the Northeast project?

The Northeast Project is China’s national research project on the Northeast frontier that
presents all the history developed within the current Chinese border as Chinese. Recently
China has been claiming claimed that the history and culture of the Korean Peninsula,
including the old Goguryeo and Balhae regions which is Korean History, belongs to China.
