Plot
In 108 B.C. Gojoseon was destroyed by the Han Dynasty. Yoo-Hwa, the daughter of the Habaek people, fell in love with Haemosu from Gojoseon, who was being chased by the enemies and hid him from the Han Dynasty and gives birth to Jumong. When Jumong grew up, he met the beautiful Soseono of the Zolbon Gyeru group, and Jumong left Buyeo and established the nation ‘Goguryeo’. In the end, Goguryeo united with Buyeo, escaped from under control of the Han Dynasty.

History of Jumong

1. Legend of Goguryeo's founding king : Jumong

Jumong's father is Haemosu, the son of Cheonje, and Yoohwa, the daughter of Habaek, who is the goddess the water. Jumong, who was good at archery established Goguryeo in 37 B.C. in Zolboncheon Stream. When his son Yuri visited Goguryeo in 19 B.C, Jumong appointed Yuri as a King and died in the same year. And Soseono, who was once the wife of Jumong went to the south and established a new country. This new countries will be the beginning of the Three Kingdoms period in Korea.

2. Early Politics and Society of Goguryeo

Goguryeo initially led politics by the royal family, Ko, and a noble family from the fifth division, united with the king. The heads of each department decided on important affairs of the country at the meeting. Each of the five divisions governed their territory with autonomy. Later, when Goguryeo grew rapidly, Buyeo demanded a hostage exchange or military invasion.

A fly in a ointment

1. Stone lanterns used as props

Stone lanterns appeared in history after accepting Buddhism. At the time of the background of this drama, Buddhism was not accepted at the time.

2. Mopalmo, a blacksmith under Jumong, discovered the secret of the chogangbup(method of making iron weapons) and produced stronger iron weapons than the Han Dynasty.

It is possible that chogangbup(method of making iron weapons) was developed in and mastered the manufacturing method around the founding of Goguryeo. However, it was in the late 2nd century A.D. and it was not until the early 4th century that the Iron Age emerged.
