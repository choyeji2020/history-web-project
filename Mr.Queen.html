Error in Using Language

Problem here is that during the Joseon Dynasty, people did not communicate in Chinese, and our ancestors never talked, thought, or expressed in the current Chinese language. Chinese characters were used, but the usage of it was totally different from that of China.

What language did the Joseon Dynasty speak?

Classical Chinese was the primary written language in Joseon but only for the elite and the wealthy. And Sejong the Great, the fourth king of the Joseon dynasty, personally created Hangul and revealed it in 1443. And since then, Hangul was widely used among Korean people.

Disparaging the Annals of the Joseon Dynasty as 'tabloids'

Problem here is that chracter in the drama says “the Annals of the Joseon Dynasty are nothing but tabloids”. And tabloid is a newpaper with vulgar gossip stories. This is degrading Korean history, including the 500-year-old Annals of the Joseon Dynasty

What is the Annals of the Joseon Dynasty?

The Joseon wangjo sillok (朝鮮王朝實錄 Veritable Records of the Joseon Dynasty) includes 28 different sets of chronological records, and each set covers one ruler's reign. It was compiled immediately after the death of the ruler in question. As such, the Veritable Records are not the typical history planned and written by a specific individual or team of individuals. The collection covers the reigns of 25 rulers, from King Taejo to King Cheoljong, and spans a period of 472 year.
