View History of Daejoyoung

Balhae Establishment Process

The father-Daejoyeong and son-Daejoongsang are from Goguryeo. In 696 A.D. they took the Goguryeo refugees to the east to build a castle with Geolsabiu and protested against the Chinese Dang Dynasty. After that, Daejoongsang and Geolsabiu died, and Daejoyeong led the group to establish a country in Dongmosan Mountain.

A fly in a ointment

Geolsabiu cannot be Daechoyeong's brother nor subordinate.

According to Sindangseo, Wu Zetian(the chinese queen) holds a meeting by conferring titles to Geolsabiu and Geoljungsang to interrupt the founding of Balhae. This means that the most important person in the founding of Balhae was not Daejoyeong. Geolgeoljungsang can be seen as a soldier around the founding of Balhae.
